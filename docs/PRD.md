# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Bebilush

## 1. Product Vision
**Bebilush** is a premium digital ecosystem for Albanian-speaking mothers (in the Balkans and Diaspora, specifically Germany). It aims to be the go-to companion for motherhood, offering tools, personalized content, and a supportive community.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- **Cultural Connection:** Strictly Albanian language content to preserve heritage.
- **Modern Convenience:** AI-powered tools to simplify parenting tasks (naming, entertainment).
- **Community:** A safe space for mothers to connect (future phase).

## 2. Target Audience
- **Primary:** Albanian-speaking mothers (New moms, expectant moms).
- **Segments:**
    - **Balkans (Albania, Kosovo, Macedonia):** Looking for modern resources and community.
    - **Diaspora (Germany, etc.):** Looking for cultural connection, advice on raising bilingual kids, and products not available locally.

## 3. Key Features (MVP & Beyond)

### A. AI-Powered Tools (The "Hook")
1.  **Baby Name Generator (Gjeni Emrin):**
    -   **Input:** Gender, origin preference (Illyrian, Muslim, Catholic, Modern), meaning keywords.
    -   **AI Integration:** Google Gemini to generate names with meanings and cultural context.

    -   **Offerings:** Custom Lullabies, Bedtime Stories, Animated Videos.

    -   **Monetization:** One-time purchase per creation.

### B. Digital Marketplace (Dyqani)
-   **Products:** eBooks (Guides for new moms in Germany vs Balkans), Checklists (Hospital bag, Vaccination), Templates.
-   **Monetization:** Direct sales.
-   **Pricing Strategy:** PPP (Purchasing Power Parity) - distinct pricing for Diaspora vs Balkans.

### C. Community (Bebilush Chat) - *Future/Teaser*
-   A space for Q&A and connection.
-   MVP: Waitlist or "Ask a Question" feature powered by AI (Gemini) as a preliminary "Expert".

## 4. AI Integrations (Google AI Studio)
-   **Gemini 2.5 Flash/Pro:** For generating names, stories, and answering parenting questions.
-   **Imagen / Nano Banana:** For generating cover art for stories or visualizing concepts.
-   **Veo:** (Future) For animating baby photos into cartoons.
-   **Audio Spark:** (Future) For text-to-speech narration of stories.

## 5. Design & User Experience
-   **Aesthetics:** Premium, warm, trustworthy. Soft pastels mixed with vibrant accents. Glassmorphism and smooth animations.
-   **Platform:** Web App (Responsive), optimized for mobile.
-   **Language:** **Strictly Albanian.**

## 6. Monetization Model
-   **Freemium Tools:** Name generator (basic free, advanced paid?).
-   **One-time Purchases:** Digital downloads, Personalized songs/videos.
-   **Subscription (Future):** Access to exclusive community and premium content.

## 7. Success Metrics
-   User engagement with the Name Generator.
-   Conversion rate on Digital Products.
-   Sign-ups for the Community waitlist.
